The Process

SCIENTIFIC METHOD

Inquiry & Hypothesis

It starts with a question. How does the microbial terroir of local grains influence the flavor profile of a miso? We formulate a hypothesis to guide our culinary exploration.

01. INQUIRY

Inoculation & Incubation

The precise introduction of Aspergillus oryzae or Saccharomyces. We create a controlled environment—a cradle for our microbial collaborators to thrive and multiply.

02. INOCULATION

Metabolic Transformation

The alchemy of time and temperature. Complex starches break down into simple sugars, proteins into amino acids (umami), and sugars into alcohols and acids. We monitor this invisible dance.

03. METABOLISM

Sensory & Chemical Analysis

The proof is in the palate and the data. We evaluate the organoleptic properties—taste, aroma, texture—alongside pH and brix readings to refine the formula for the next generation.

04. ANALYSIS
